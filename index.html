Breast Cancer

Breast cancer is a cancer that develops in the cells of the breast. Breast cancer is still the most invase in women.

View details »

Signs And Symptoms Of Breast Cancer

A breast lump or thickening that feels different from the surrounding tissue
Change in the size, shape or appearance of a breast
Changes to the skin over the breast, such as dimpling
A newly inverted nipple
Peeling, scaling, crusting or flaking of the pigmented area of skin surrounding the nipple (areola) or breast skin
Redness or pitting of the skin over your breast, like the skin of an orange

Below Are Five Ways To Treat Breat Cancer

Surgery. An operation where doctors cut out cancer tissue.
Chemotherapy. Using special medicines to shrink or kill the cancer cells.
Hormonal therapy. Blocks cancer cells from getting the hormones they need to grow.
Biological therapy.
Radiation therapy.

Teenage Pregnancy

Teenage Pregnancy also known as Adolescent pregnancy is pregnancy in girls who are under the age of 20.

View details »

Causes Of Teenage Pregnancy

Drugs and Alcohol.
Low Socio-economic Status.
Peer Pressure and Sexual Abuse.
Media Influence.
Mothers.
Child.
Family and Peer Support.
Education/Knowledge.

Tips For Parents To Help Their Children Avoid Teen Pregnancy

Be clear about your own sexual values and attitudes. Talk with your children early and often about sex and love. Be specific. Know your children’s friends and their families. Know what your kids are watching, reading and listening to.
